About the Project

Engineering Focus

Decisions driven by technical accuracy, flight dynamics, and aircraft systems modeling.

Historical Accuracy

Recreating Polish Su-22M4 cockpit, instrumentation, and operational procedures.

Educational Goal

Non-commercial project focused on FlightGear community, learning, and research.

Project Goals

Accurate Flight Dynamics

JSBSim-based aerodynamic and propulsion modeling

Detailed Aircraft Systems

Electrical, fuel, hydraulic, and avionics architecture

Historically Consistent Cockpit

Layout, instrumentation, and operational procedures

Engineering-First Approach

No simplifications driven by gameplay

Project Status

Under Active Development

The aircraft is currently not publicly released. Core systems and flight model foundations are being established.

Collaboration

Working closely with museums, archival researchers, former pilots, and technical specialists to ensure accuracy.
